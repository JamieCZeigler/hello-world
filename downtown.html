The Heros of Downtown Harrisonburg

Downtown Harrisonburg is rich with culture. The community has come together to build something wonderful for the people who live here. The local Nonprofits are just one aspect of the community that devotes their time to making Harrisnburg a great and respectful place to live. One organization, The Art Council, has events for the community every month (First Friday's), and works effortlessly restoring the arcetecture and natural beauty of downtown.

Although the downtown area has turned around significantly in the last 10 years, there are still many issues that people struggle with. Organizations such as United Way, The Community Foundation, Boys & Girls Club and many more, work together to help combat transportation, childcare and housing for the community members.
