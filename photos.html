Photography

Once the hospital in Harrisonburg, JMU Students get to walk the halls of the iconic Students Success Center.

In the graduate building there is a lovely mural that lines the halls.

One of the local nonprofits, New Creations, aims to stop Human Traficing. All of their proceeds go towards their mission.

The Student Success Center is home to the Community Service Learning office as well as many other resources for students to be involved in the community.

There are many nonprofits downtown where you can support a good cause while shopping for yourself or loved ones.
